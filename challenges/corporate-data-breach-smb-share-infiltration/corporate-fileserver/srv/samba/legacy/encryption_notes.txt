Legacy System Encryption Documentation
======================================

Cipher Implementation: Dual Layer Security
1. ROT13 substitution cipher (13 character rotation)
2. Base64 encoding for obfuscation

Decryption Process:
- First decode Base64
- Then apply ROT13 decryption

Example:
'Uryyb' (ROT13) -> 'Hello'
'SGVsbG8=' (Base64) -> 'Hello'
