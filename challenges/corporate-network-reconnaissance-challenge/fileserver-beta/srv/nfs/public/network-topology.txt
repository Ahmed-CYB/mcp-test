Scanning Strategy:
1. Use nmap for comprehensive port discovery
2. Check for service banners and versions
3. Look for hidden or non-standard ports
4. Enumerate shares and accessible resources
5. Combine information from all three systems

Flag Fragment Location Hints:
- Alpha fragment: Hidden in web service headers
- Beta fragment: Accessible through file shares
- Gamma fragment: Database service enumeration

Combine all three fragments to form: CTF{alpha_beta_gamma}
